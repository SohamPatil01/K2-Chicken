### 📱 **1. Customer Experience Enhancements**

#### **A. Real-Time Order Tracking**

- **Live Order Status Updates**: Real-time order tracking with visual progress bar
  - Status indicators: Confirmed → Preparing → Ready → Out for Delivery → Delivered
  - Estimated time remaining with countdown timer
  - Map view showing delivery driver location (if applicable)
  - Push notifications for status changes
  - SMS/Email notifications for order updates

#### **B. Enhanced Product Discovery**

- **Advanced Search & Filters**:
  - Search by product name, category, price range

  - Sort by: price (low to high), popularity, newest, bestsellers
  - "Recently Viewed" products section
  - "Frequently Bought Together" suggestions
  - "You May Also Like" recommendations based on cart

#### **C. Wishlist/Favorites**

- Save favorite products for quick reordering
- Create custom lists (e.g., "Weekly Groceries", "Party Order")

- Quick add from wishlist to cart

- Side-by-side product comparison
- Compare prices, weights, nutritional info (if available)
- Highlight differences between similar products

#### **E. Product Reviews & Ratings**

- **Enhanced Review System**:
  - Star ratings (1-5) for each product
  - Photo reviews (customers can upload images)
  - Verified purchase badges
  - Helpful/Not helpful voting on reviews
  - Sort reviews by: newest, highest rated, most helpful
  - Filter by rating (5 stars, 4 stars, etc.)
  - Review responses from admin

#### **F. Quick Reorder**

- "Reorder Last Order" button
- Order history with one-click reorder
- Save favorite combinations as "Meal Sets"
- Scheduled recurring orders (weekly/monthly)
